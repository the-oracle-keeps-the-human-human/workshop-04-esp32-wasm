🦖 Round 2 — Digimon Desk-Pets

Round 1 ran tiny .wasm on the chip. Round 2: ship a desk-pet character pack — your own Digimon, animated on a real ESP32-S3, flashable from the browser. Same data-file flow, so it self-merges.

🛠️ Add yours — just PR the code (one folder, self-merges)

  1. Copy submissions/_TEMPLATE/ → submissions/NN-<yourname>/ and fill the README.
  2. Drop your 7 state GIFs (96×100) in characters/<id>/ and the preview copies in docs/preview/gifs/<id>/.
  3. Build a <id>-storage.bin (LittleFS with your character) — see the template's build-storage note.
  4. Add docs/manifest-<id>.json (the desk-pet image) and docs/packs/<id>.json (with previewDir + states).
  5. Open a PR. CI (flasher-check) verifies the offset-0 part is 0xE9 (no bricks), then it self-merges and appears on the flasher + preview automatically.

Copy a working example: packs/tonk.json + manifest-tonk.json.
