# weizen pack — provenance & license

A **weissbier glass** desk-pet — cloudy gold wheat beer, cream foam, a face on the beer.
"Unfiltered Weizen": the yeast stays in the glass. Same `manifest.json` schema as bufo/cat/clawd,
**96×100** frames, the seven canonical states, one AnimatedGIF decoder.

## Source art & license

| Asset | Source | License |
|---|---|---|
| 7 weizen animations (96×100 GIFs) | **100% original** — hand-drawn here in pure Python/Pillow (`gen_weizen_pack.py`), no upstream sprites | **MIT** |

No third-party art. Every pixel is generated by the committed script, so the pack rebuilds
deterministically and carries no external license obligation.

| dizzy | dizzy.gif | swirling eyes + spinning marks |
| heart | heart.gif | `^_^` + hearts floating up |

## How it was built (`gen_weizen_pack.py`)

Pure stdlib + Pillow on a headless VM (no ImageMagick/gifsicle needed). Each state is drawn at
48×50, upscaled ×2 NEAREST → 96×100 chunky pixels, saved as optimized GIF89a (≤16 frames) on the
manifest bg `#15110B`. Rebuild: `python3 gen_weizen_pack.py`.
